CROP RECOMMENDATION AND SOIL ANALYSIS

Crop recommendation and soil analysis benefit from machine learning algorithms in agriculture. For crop recommendation, methods like Random Forest, Decision Tree. In soil analysis, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) excel at extracting features from soil images, enabling accurate assessment of soil health and quality based on visual data. Ultimately aim of this project is to increase crop yields.
